Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Mentora Edge (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of students, parents, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Student name, class, school, and contact details
  • Parent/guardian name and contact information
  • Email address and phone number
  • Fee payment and enrollment records
  • Academic records, test scores, and progress reports
  • Login credentials for dashboards (securely stored)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage admissions, classes, and student records
  • To provide study materials, dashboards, and progress tracking
  • To communicate announcements and important updates
  • To share academic progress reports with parents/guardians
  • For internal administrative and academic purposes

3. Academic & Progress Data

Weekly or periodic test progress reports are generated for academic monitoring. These reports are intended to be reviewed by parents or guardians to ensure transparency and improvement.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. Access to sensitive information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Sharing of Information

Mentora Edge does not sell or rent personal data. Information may only be shared:

  • With parents or guardians for academic purposes
  • If required by law or legal authorities
  • For essential service operations (secure platforms only)

6. Cookies & Website Data

Our website may use basic cookies or local storage to improve user experience, login sessions, and website functionality. No unnecessary tracking is performed.

7. Children’s Privacy

Since our services involve minors, all data collection is done with the knowledge and consent of parents or guardians. We prioritize child data protection and responsible handling.

8. Policy Updates

Mentora Edge reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be communicated through official channels or the website.

9. Contact Us

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or data handling practices, please contact us via the official Mentora Edge communication channels.
