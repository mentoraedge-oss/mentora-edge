About Mentora Edge

Shaping Minds. Sharpening Futures.

Mentora Edge is an academic learning platform dedicated to providing structured, concept-driven education for CBSE students of Classes VIII, IX and X.

Founded by experienced educators with over two decades of teaching excellence, Mentora Edge focuses on clarity, consistency, and confidence building — not rote learning.

Our mission is to guide students through a disciplined learning journey while empowering them to think critically and perform confidently.

From the Managing Director’s Desk

At Mentora Edge, we believe that education is not merely about marks, but about building clarity, confidence, and a strong academic foundation that lasts a lifetime.

As a student myself, I understand the challenges learners face today. Mentora Edge was founded with a vision to provide structured guidance, disciplined learning, and concept clarity at the right stage of a student’s academic journey.

With experienced educators and a learner-first approach, our goal is to nurture confidence, consistency, and curiosity — enabling students to perform academically while growing personally.

Abir Munshi

Founder & Managing Director
